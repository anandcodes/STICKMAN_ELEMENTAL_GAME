## Play Store Assets for Elemental Stickman

Use this file as a guide when preparing your final Google Play listing.

### Required graphics

- **Adaptive launcher icon**
  - Background and foreground layers following Play guidelines.
  - Style: clean silhouette of the elemental stickman with fire/water/earth/wind motifs.
- **Screenshots**
  - At least 3–5 phone screenshots:
    - Main menu showing campaign + endless modes.
    - Mid-level combat with multiple elements.
    - Shop and relic selection screens.
    - Victory or high-score/leaderboard screen.
- **Feature graphic**
  - 1024×500 (or current Play requirement).
  - Show the hero, elemental effects, and a portal/level backdrop.

### Text content

- **Title**
  - “Elemental Stickman: Element Wars” (example – adjust as desired).
- **Short description**
  - One concise sentence highlighting elemental combat and dual modes.
- **Full description**
  - 3–6 short paragraphs or bullet lists covering:
    - Core gameplay loop (platforming, elemental casting).
    - Campaign and endless modes.
    - Upgrades, relics, and trials.
    - Accessibility features (text scale, high contrast, reduced motion).
    - Localization (English + Hindi).

### Store metadata

- **Privacy Policy URL**
  - Host the finalized version of `PRIVACY_POLICY_TEMPLATE.md` on your own site and paste the URL here.
- **Category & tags**
  - Game → Action/Platformer (or closest fit).
- **Content rating**
  - Complete the Play questionnaire based on actual in-game content (combat, mild fantasy violence).

### Release process

- Build an **Android App Bundle (AAB)** from the Android project.
- Upload to an **internal test** track first.
- Invite testers and collect feedback + crash reports.
- Roll out to **closed** then **production** with staged rollout.
