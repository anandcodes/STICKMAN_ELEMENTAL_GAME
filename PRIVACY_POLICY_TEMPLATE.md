## Elemental Stickman – Privacy & Data Use (Template)

This is a **template** privacy policy for development and testing. Before publishing to Google Play, you must review it with your own legal counsel and replace it with a finalized policy, hosted on your own website.

### 1. Data we collect

- **Gameplay analytics** (optional, configurable in Settings):
  - Anonymous events about sessions, levels played, deaths, victories, and other gameplay outcomes.
  - Approximate device information (such as browser/OS version and screen size).
- **Error reports**:
  - Anonymous crash and error information, including error messages and stack traces.
- **Progression & leaderboards**:
  - Your in-game progress (levels completed, scores, gems, achievements).
  - Nickname and score for leaderboards, if you choose to submit them.

We do **not** collect your real name, email address, phone number, or precise location from inside the game.

### 2. How we use data

- To keep the game running reliably (debugging crashes and errors).
- To balance levels and improve future updates.
- To operate leaderboards and progression across devices (where enabled).

### 3. Third parties

- **Google AdMob** (ads):
  - Shows ads in the Android version and may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s policies.
- **Google Play Billing**:
  - Processes payments for in-app purchases (such as “Remove Ads”).
- **Backend services** (if configured):
  - Our own servers may store leaderboard entries and progression data.

You should link to:
- Google’s privacy policy and AdMob policy pages.
- Any backend or analytics providers you use.

### 4. Your choices

- You can disable **anonymous analytics** in the in-game Settings under “Data & Privacy”.
- You can purchase **“Remove Ads”** (where available) to turn off in-game ads on this device.
